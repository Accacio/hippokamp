* Identities
- $\sin^2(\alpha)+\cos^2(\alpha)=1$
- $\sin(\alpha+\beta)=\sin(\alpha)$
- $\sin(\alpha)\sin(\beta)={1\over 2}(\cos(\alpha-\beta)-\cos(\alpha+\beta))$
